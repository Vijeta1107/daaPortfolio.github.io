Course Project Introduction

RAJPUR

WELCOME TO OUR TEAM PORTFOLIO ON SMART CITY PROJECT

Problem Space:

Cities are growing rapidly, leading to challenges in infrastructure, resource management, and urban living quality. The Smart City Rajpur project aims to address these challenges and enhance urban living by integrating technology to improve efficiency, sustainability, and quality of life.

White Paper Findings:

Simple Tech Solutions: Ideas like IoT-based smart meters for monitoring water usage, automated waste management systems, and smart traffic management for reducing congestion and pollution.

City Design Recommendations:

  • Upgrade outdated infrastructure like roads, water pipes, and buildings.
  • Implement smart grids for energy efficiency and renewable energy sources.
  • Develop green spaces and encourage eco-friendly transportation options like electric buses and bike lanes.
  • Integrate smart technologies like sensors for waste management, traffic monitoring, and environmental quality measurements.

Problem Definition:

The goal is to transform Rajpur into a smart city by utilizing technology to address urban challenges, improve the quality of life for residents, and ensure the city's sustainability for future generations. This includes developing systems that support efficient resource management, enhance safety, and improve urban services.
